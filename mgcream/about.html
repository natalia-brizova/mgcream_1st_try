About us

After five years of development, we are honoured to introduce our magnesium cream MgCream.

Magnesium is one of the essential minerals of the human body - an integral part of well-functioning biological processes and the entire organism. It is indispensable for the nervous system, muscles, joints, bones, and in the body’s daily process of regeneration.

We are delighted to have developed a cream whose composition is globally unique. By blending a carefully selected composition of bioactive substances, MgCream has been designed for the effective absorption and utilization of magnesium in the body. Local supplementation of magnesium supports regeneration at the point of need. MgCream’s uniqueness is also reflected in the absence of PEG emulsifiers.

We believe that the remarkably versatile MgCream will help you live with ease and comfort.
